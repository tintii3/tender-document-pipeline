6. Results

Value of all Contracts Awarded in this Notice: 18,874,960 Euros

Direct Award: Yes

Reason for Direct Award: The contract can only be executed by a specific economic operator due to technical reasons, no competition exists.

Other Justifications: The solution proposed by Multiwave Technologies (MGNTQ) is the only mobile ultra-low field MRI scanner that can image multiple parts of the human body and is sufficiently adaptable for our studies. The MGNTQ scanner has the following key characteristics: it is lightweight (less than 230kg), operates at ultra-low field (close to 50mT), does not require specific rooms/equipment, has a Hallbach array magnet with a diameter larger than 25cm for enhanced performance, and comes with multiple spectrometers. These features are essential for our project, which aims to exploit the mobility of the device to bring MRI technology to rural and/or remote areas where access to imaging is difficult.
